The Queen of Melody

(28 September 1929 - 06 February 2022)

Her Life

Family of Lata Mangeshkar

Born:

28 September 1929, Indore, MP

Real Name:

Hema Mangeshkar

Parents:

Pandit Deenanath Mangeshkar and Shevanti

Siblings:

Meena, Asha, Usha and Hridaynath

Career

1942:

Began her career as an actor and singer due to the demise of her father.

1942-48:

Acted in over eight films.

1943:

Her first Hindi song was Mata Ek Sapoot Ki Duniya Badal De Tu for the movie Gajaabhaau.

1945:

Started classical music training from Ustad Aman Ali Khan.

1949:

First major hit was Aayega Aanewala from the movie Mahal.

1955:

Composed music for the first time for Ram Ram Pavhane (Marathi Movie).

1974:

Became the first Indian Artist to perform at the Royal Albert Hall.

1989:

Founded Lata Mangeshkar Medical Foundation, along with her siblings.
